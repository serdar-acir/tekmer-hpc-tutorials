# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## How do I open a TEKMER HPC account?
1. If you or your organization is not yet registered, please first contact TEKMER to initiate registration:  
   https://tuzlatekmer.com/iletisim  
2. Visit the user portal:  
   https://tekmer.performetrica.com/  
3. Click on **"Online Registration"** and complete the form.  
4. Follow the instructions sent to your email to activate your account.

For any issues, contact: contact@performetrica.com

---

## How long does it take to get started?
If your email domain or organization is already approved, you can start using the system immediately after completing registration.

Otherwise, approval time depends on TEKMER evaluation.

---

## I am an individual. Can I use the cluster?
Yes. Individual users may be accepted if their work is within a research and development scope.

All applications are subject to approval by TEKMER.

---

## Our organization is registered. Do all users need separate approval?
No. Once an organization is registered, its members can self-register using their organizational email addresses.

---

## How do I close my HPC account?
Send an email to: contact@performetrica.com  
Include your username and request for account termination.

---

## My software is not available in the cluster. What can I do?
Submit a software request via the user portal:  
https://tekmer.performetrica.com/

Use the **"Software Request"** section.

Requests are evaluated based on:
- Licensing constraints
- System compatibility
- Resource impact

---

## I applied for an account. What happens next?
If your application is approved, you will receive your credentials via email.

If additional verification is required, you may be contacted.

---

## What are the rules for using TEKMER HPC Center?
Please refer to the **TEKMER HPC Usage Policy** document for detailed rules, including:
- Acceptable use
- Resource allocation
- Security policies
- User responsibilities
