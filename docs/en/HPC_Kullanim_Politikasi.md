# TEKMER HPC Cluster Usage Policy

All users of the TEKMER HPC infrastructure agree to comply with the following rules.

---

## 1. Eligibility

- Start-ups and researchers within TEKMER may use the HPC system.
- External users may apply if they are involved in academic projects affiliated with a Turkish university.
- All applications are subject to approval by TEKMER.

---

## 2. Account Management

- Accounts are created and managed by the group supervisor (PI) via the HPC portal.
- The PI confirms that users have basic Linux and HPC knowledge.
- After activation, users gain access to HPC resources.

Users can:

- Manage passwords  
- Submit jobs  
- View usage statistics  
- Access announcements and support  

via the portal.

---

## 3. Access

- Access is provided via SSH.
- VPN access may be required depending on security policies.
- All access is subject to TEKMER IT policies.

---

## 4. Software Usage

Users may run:

- Licensed HPC-compatible software  
- Custom-developed parallel applications  

New software must be requested via the portal or email prior to use.

---

## 5. Data Management

- The HPC system is **not intended for long-term data storage**.
- Users must:

    - Maintain backups  
    - Remove data after use  

System administrators may delete files older than **30 days** without prior notice if needed.

---

## 6. Job Submission and Resource Usage

- All jobs must be submitted via the job scheduler (e.g., Slurm).
- Running compute jobs on login nodes is prohibited.
- Resources are allocated based on fair-use principles.

---

## 7. Account Activity

- Accounts are considered active projects without a fixed end date.
- Users must confirm activity every **3 months**.
- Inactive accounts may be removed.

---

Users are responsible for:

- Protecting their credentials

## 11. Prohibited Activities

The following are strictly prohibited:

- Illegal activities  
- Actions compromising system security  
- Unauthorized commercial usage  
- Security testing or attacks on other systems  

---

## 12. Sensitive Data

Sensitive data (e.g., national security, confidential IP) may only be processed with explicit approval.

---

## 13. Access Control

- Users may access only their own data.
- Unauthorized access to other users’ data is strictly prohibited.
- Monitoring mechanisms may be in place.

---

## 14. Enforcement

Violation of this policy may result in:

- Temporary suspension  
- Permanent account termination
